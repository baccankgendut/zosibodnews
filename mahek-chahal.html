Mahek Chahal is a Norwegian actor, model, dancer, and television personality who has primarily worked in the Indian entertainment industry and has been cast in Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Telugu, and Kannada films such as Main Aurr Mrs Khanna, Nayee Padosan, Bikram Singha: The Lion Is Back, Mr. Airavata, Marega Salaa, Chameli, Chhodon Naa Yaar, Karar: The Deal, Dil Apna Punjabi, Karar: The Deal, Gethu, Jai Veeru, Yamla Pagla Deewana, Nirdosh, Wanted, Jatt Airways, Kedi, Neetho, and Anjaan. In addition to that, she has also appeared as a contestant on shows like Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi 11, Bigg Boss 5, Bigg Boss Halla Bol, Power Couple, Kahani Comedy Circus Ki, and Killer Karaoke Atka Toh Latkah.
