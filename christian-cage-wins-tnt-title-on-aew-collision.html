Christian Cage Wins TNT Title On AEW Collision

September 04, 2024
post

AEW brought a loaded episode of Collision this week, and they started off with a TNT Title match. During that match, Darby Allin fought for the TNT Title in a triple threat match against both Christian Cage and Luchasaurus. That match ended with a title change.

Christian Cage has carried the TNT Title for Luchasaurus since he became champion. On this week’s Collision, Cage won the title and made that ownership official.

Darby Allin hit his Coffin Drop on Luchasaurus to close out the match, but Cage threw Darby out of the ring. Then Christian secured the pinfall and won the match.

After the match was over, Christian celebrated big time, but Luchasaurus didn’t look quite as happy. Either way, Darby Allin was robbed of another title run in AEW.

This was quite an exciting way to begin AEW Collision this week. The fans didn’t seem to appreciate this title change, but Christian Cage is also at the top of his game when it comes to heel work.
