Rene Casados was born on October 21, 1961 (age 62) in Mexico. He is a Celebrity Soap Opera Actor. Though he appeared in close to twenty telenovelas between the late 1970s and the early 2000s, this actor is perhaps most famous for his roles as Father Tadeo and Bruno Mendizaba in, respectively, Fuego En Le Sangre and La Madrastra.
