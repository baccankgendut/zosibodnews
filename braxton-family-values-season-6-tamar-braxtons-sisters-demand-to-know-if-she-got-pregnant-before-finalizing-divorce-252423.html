Braxton Family Values will soon be returning for Season 6, and the trailer indicates that the Braxton women are finally facing the issues they have with each other. Fans can also expect to see a lot of changes in both their professional and private lives.

The trailer kicks off with a therapy session led by celebrity life coach Iyanla Vanzant, and it’s obvious the women are unhappy, with Toni declaring that she doesn’t like her family and Vanzant calling the situation a “hotbed of crazy.”

https://www.instagram.com/p/Buc-jLABBfO/

According to OK! Magazine, this season viewers will get the chance to see highlights from Tamar and Traci’s mini-tour late last year, and there will also be some exclusive footage from Tamar’s victory party after winning Celebrity Big Brother.

The trailer also includes a scene where Tamar drops a hint that she could be pregnant. But, when Trina straight up asks her sister if she is expecting, Tamar dodges the question while her divorce from Vince Herbert remains in limbo.

In a recent interview, Tamar said that her relationship with Herbert is better than ever, and she is glad she has her friend back. Even though the end of their marriage was rough, fans saw it all play out on Tamar & Vince, and their divorce is still not final, Tamar says that they have worked hard to repair their friendship and she is grateful for that.

“Vince and I don’t work together anymore, but I’m glad I got my friend back. Our relationship is great,” she said. “Obviously, I never thought I’d be in this situation, but I think it’s for the best. I think there’s hope that he’s happy and definitely hope for my own happiness because us together and working together was completely toxic. Our marriage had absolutely no hope after a few years of working together, the way we was working together.”

https://www.instagram.com/p/Bt1q2R0BIqv/

Tamar Braxton has also moved on and found love again with her new boyfriend. We don’t know his name, but she jokingly calls him “T’Challa.”

The new season will also feature the ongoing drama between Toni and her longtime boyfriend, Birdman. “Maybe I’m not engaged anymore,” she tells her sisters. “I’m not ready to talk about it.”
