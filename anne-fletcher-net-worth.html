What is Anne Fletcher's Net Worth?

Anne Fletcher is an American actress, film director, and choreographer who has a net worth of $15 million. Fletcher was born in Detroit, Michigan in May 1966. She took dance lessons as a child and appeared on the show Salute to the Superstars at age 15. Anne moved to California and was trained by Joe Tremaine.
