Les Twins are French Choreographers, dancers, and models. The duo is made of two identical twins. Their names are called Larry and Laurent Bourgeois. They are best known for their incredible dancing. Their nicknames are Ca Blaze and Lil Beast.

Early Life

Les Twins were born in 1988 on 6th December. Their birthplace was Sarcelles, Paris, France. Both Laurent and Larry were born last in their family. Les Twins have seven siblings.

Career

Initially, Les Twins formed a group by enlisted five other members. They gave the group the name Criminalz Crew. The dancers could entertain people and contest for dancing competitions. During the Avignon Festival of 2007, the dance crew performed for an audience. The following year, they beat both Meech Onomo and Joseph Go. They defeated the dancers during the Juste Debout contest.

Subsequently, they became the Incroyable Talent’s finalists. Then, their video of the San Diego’s World of Dance tour went viral on YouTube on August 10. Les Twins’ U.S. television debut was on The Ellen DeGeneres Show in November that same year.

2011 was a great year for the Les Twins. They won the Juste Debout and performed together with Beyoncé in her various events of that year. Also, they appeared as dancers in Cirque du Soleil’s video. The following year, Les Twins performed with artists such as Missy Elliott, Big Sean, and Timbaland in various events.

On 24th July 2012, they made their debut on Nippon TV. That same year, Les Twins guest-starred on Everybody Dance in Ukraine. Then they collaborated with Missy Elliott at the Back2Black Festival and the Beats on the Beach event in Rio de Janeiro and Abu Dhabi, respectively. Les Twins have worked with Sony Japan and have also appeared in Avengers of Extreme Illusions.

Currently, they are signed with NEXT Model Management in Paris. In 2010, Les Twins walked the runway of Paris Fashion Week. They did it for Jean Paul Gaultier Fall for his Men’s Collection. Other campaigns that they have been part of include That’s Rocawear by Jay-Z, Givenchy, Adidas, EMOBILE, and Beats By Dre. In addition to that, Les Twins have been featured in Benetton, New Era, H&M, Pioneer Steez, 25 Magazine as well as the Hewlett-Packard.

The dancers have held world-wide dance workshops. They have visited countries such as the U.S, Canada, Germany, Poland, the Czech Republic, and Finland, among others.

Awards & Achievements

In 2010, Les Twins won the year’s Battle of the Stylez-Germany. The following year, they won Juste Debout Japan as well as the Juste Debout Finals, among others. They won both Hip Hop International and Freestyle Session 15 in 2012. Two years later, they won World of Dance Industry Awards and World of Dance All Styles. In 2017, they won the World Of Dance.
