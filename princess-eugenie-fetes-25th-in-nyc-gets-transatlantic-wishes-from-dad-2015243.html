Princess Eugenie Fetes 25th in NYC, Gets Transatlantic Wishes From Dad

September 05, 2024
post

A birthday celebration fit for a princess! Princess Eugenie of York celebrated her 25th birthday on Monday, March 23, and the day was full of surprises, well-wishes, and fun festivities.

Spending her day in New York City, the London-born royal was celebrated by her father, Prince Andrew, in a high-tech way at St. James’ Palace. The Duke of York was hosting a Pitch@Palace event, which covered entrepreneurship and business ventures, when he paused his presentation for a special surprise. 

Prince Andrew, 55, whipped out an iPad and FaceTimed his daughter to send a transatlantic birthday wish. The celebrating princess answered with her mother, Sarah Ferguson, and her sister, Princess Beatrice, on either side of her. Wearing a pair of funky birthday cake sunglasses, Eugenie waved to her father from thousands of miles away.
