Conor McGregor is heading to Las Vegas that will see him ultimately return to the octagon.

The Irishman will coach in UFC reality show The Ultimate Fighter before making his comeback where he will fight Michael Chandler later this year.

McGregor took to Instagram to share snaps alongside his family as he flew out to the United States to film the reality TV series on board his private jet.

The two-weight UFC champion was pictured in a cream tracksuit alongside his three kids and partner Dee Devlin.

McGregor’s kids can be seen watching films on their tablets and enjoying the lavish method of transport.

He also shared a video of his chicken burger bought from the Black Forge Inn, the pub in Dublin he owns.

McGregor wrote: "On my way to Vegas to coach a few kids to a UFC contract. A blessing. A privilege. An honour. My pleasure. Welcome to 'UFC with the Mac'".

The 34-year-old is bracing himself for a blockbuster return to the octagon later this year.
