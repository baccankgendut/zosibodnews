Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro Wiki Biography

Christiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on 5 February 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, and also known as Ronnie, Cris, The Sultan of the Stepover, and CR, he is one of the greatest Portuguese soccer players of all time, and actually in the world, having won four FIFA Ballon d’Or Awards, on top of four European Golden Shoes.

So just how rich is Christiano Ronaldo, as of mid-2017? Authoritative sources have estimated that Ronaldo’s net worth is as high as $420 million, accumulated during his career in top flight soccer which began in 2002 . This may well increase as Christiano’s current salary and income from playing and sponsorship deals is now close to $60 million every year, with Forbes ranking him “the world’s highest-paid athlete” in 2016 and 2017.
