Solo Battles Problem In The Latest Madden

Solo battles Madden 24 not working due to server related problems or a bad internet connection. The connectivity issue has been the primary cause. 

Many users complained about getting the “Our Servers can’t process your request at this moment.” The problem seems to be from the developer’s end.

EA Sports’s servers can sometimes take a heavy hit due to work overload. The aforementioned message occurs in such cases and prevents you from accessing this game mode.

In such a case, visiting the EA website for information and inspecting their social media pages for updates can be an ideal solution.

Likewise, some other probable causes restricting users to try this feature include:

  • Internet connection problem
  • Inactive solo battle events
  • Suspended account
  • Operating on an older game version

Sometimes, the main culprit can be your home network. A weak signal or lower internet speed can consume time and forbid you from entering the battle.

If you have missed a crucial update and your game is running on an outdated version, the problem can arise.

Similarly, accessing the game mode after the end of an event or when the battle mode is inactive won’t do you good.

You can clear the console’s cache, restart your game, and uninstall and reinstall the game to fix the problem. Or you can contact EA support for assistance.

What Does Madden Solo Battles Mean?

Solo battles in Madden is a game mode where you hand over your MUT team to Artificial Intelligence (AI) to battle against another AI-controlled team.

The idea is to discover the balance of the team. It helps analyze the overall defensive and offensive prowess of the team.

In other words, the battle is a simulation where the AI take your team and makes them battle a CPU-controlled side.

At the beginning of the contest, you are given a chance to see the opponent’s team and make the necessary adjustments. After that, the teams battle it out in a full-length match.

You can decide the difficulty level and play accordingly. But remember that the higher the difficulty level, the greater the possibility for more rewards.

The number of games available against the AI is limited. You can contest against a CPU team only once and attempt to gather as many points as possible.

For example, Madden 22 allowed thirteen games weekly. Its format was:

  • Featured opponent: 1 game to be played anytime during the week
  • Monday, Wednesday, and Friday: 4 opponents playable for one time without repetition
