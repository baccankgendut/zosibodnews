Ryan has been annihilated from the Big Brother House

August 17, 2024
post

Annihilation week has finally come to an end this evening, but before that, one final twist was delivered to the Big Brother House.

In tonight’s live eviction show, host Emma Willis entered the House as the housemates took part in a live ‘eviction showdown’.

Once all the housemates were standing behind their podiums, Emma then revealed which six housemates received the most votes from the public and that would not be facing annihilation.

The three housemates with the most votes from the public vote was Laura, Ryan and Sam. After their names were called out, they were then asked to stand behind podiums labelled 1,2, and 3.

Emma then told the safe housemates that they have to decide which housemate gets evicted. In envelopes in front of them, housemates were asked to write the name of the housemate they wished to evict.

After each housemate revealed who they wished to evict, Emma then revealed that Ryan received the most votes from the housemates and would be annihilated.

Big Brother continues tomorrow night on Channel 5.
