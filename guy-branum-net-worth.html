Early Career and Breakthrough

Guy Branum‘s career in the entertainment industry began with a unique blend of humor and intellect. After completing his education, Branum moved to San Francisco, where he was hired as a writer for Unscrewed with Martin Sargent on TechTV.

Transition to Television and Writing

Branum’s talent as a writer and performer quickly caught the attention of other networks. He became a writer and producer for G4tv.com and later the head writer for the G4 channel program X-Play.
