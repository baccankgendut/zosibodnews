Fortnite

As mentioned, Darren is best known for working on Fortnite, a 2017 online video game available as separate software packages, and which achieved overwhelming success in the following year. It has several modes, such as “Fortnite: Save the World”, “Fortnite: Battle Royale” and “Fortnite: Creative”, with the first and the second mode being released in 2017. The first mode includes up to four players who fight zombie-like creatures, and the second one is a free battle royale game which can include up to 100 players. The operative systems that can be used to play the game include Windows, macOS, Android, Switch and PlayStation 4. Fortnite Battle Royale soon became the most successful mode, earning the company hundreds of millions of dollars per month, ultimately becoming a culture phenomenon, and often seen in the mainstream media.
